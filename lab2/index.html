Trophies

Very few clubs anywhere in the world have won so many titles. The club's greatest pride and joy remain the four European Cup titles won in Wembley (1992, 2011), Paris (2006), Rome (2009), Berlin (2015) the FIFA Club World Cup (2009, 2011 and 2015)

Records

World Year with most official titles: 2009, with 6: Copa del Rey, Liga, Champions League, Spanish Super Cup, European Super Cup and Club World Cup. Team with most international trophies: 22 (3 Club World Cups, 5 Champions League, 4 Cup Winners' Cups, 3 Fairs Cups, 5 European Super Cups, 2 Latin Cups)
